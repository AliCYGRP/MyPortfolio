About Me

My name is Syed Ali Hasan, a final year student pursuing B.Tech in Information Technology from Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology, (GGSIPU), Rohini. I have experience in software development with C++ and Android by working on various personal and commercial projects throughout my journey. I have worked with Boost C++ (finest open-source C++ libraries) as a part of Google Summer of Code 2020 to develop their Boost.Astronomy Library, with GAIL (Gas Authority India Limited), Aztek Solutions, Noida, and IOSD (International Organisation of Software Developers) to develop Android Application based solutions that majorly used Java, XML, SQL, and various Android Components and practices. Besides this, I have sound knowledge of Data Structures and Algorithms. I also love interacting and brainstorming on creative ideas. My resume, here, expands on my experiences and campus involvement.
